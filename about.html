Trade Up
Orange Television Network
Trade Up is a student run, produced, directed, and created program, that is created in the Orange Televsion Network (OTN) offices. Our clips will be available on Youtube in a couple of weeks. We discuss the latest media trends impacting the industry today. We tackle topics such as HULU's live streaming service, the Sundance Film Festival, and AT&Ts direcTV, in addition to a round up of the week's major media news events. We welcome viewer participation, so contact us on Twitter and let us know your thoughts.
OTN is the student TV station at Syracuse University and was the first college cable TV station in the country to go high-def! On the air since 2005, OTN is student produced TV and it is on the air 24/7 throughout the school year. Our student crews produce comedy, sports, dance, music and artistic performances all over campus that have you as the star. We are not afraid to say it: It's all about you!
